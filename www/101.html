SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

101 – AUTOMATIC EXTERNAL DEFIBRILLATOR

Assessment

  • Patient in Cardiopulmonary Arrest
  • Basic Life Support in progress
  • AED in use

EMR / EMT

  1. If AED available, apply to patient and follow prompts
  2. 100% oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to patient’s condition
  3. CPR compression rate: 100/min for all ages
  4. Ventilation rate: 8–10/min with no pause in CPR for ventilation
  5. Continue CPR per AHA Healthcare Provider Guidelines
  6. If AED is already in use, allow shocks to complete, then check pulse
  7. If no pulse: continue CPR and basic life support
  8. If pulse present: evaluate respirations and provide supportive care

AEMT

  1. IV NS Bolus (20 cc/kg) then TKO

Paramedic

  1. Monitor patient and treat per SOP specific for the arrhythmia

Notes

  • AED is relatively contraindicated in the following situations:
    • Victim in standing water – move, dry chest and surroundings
    • Trauma Cardiac Arrest
    • Implanted pacemaker – place pads 1 inch from device
    • If ICD/AICD is delivering shock, wait 30–60 seconds before using AED
    • Transdermal medication patch at AED pad site – remove and clean area
