SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

109 – TORSADES DE POINTE

Assessment

  • Decreased / altered LOC
  • Dyspnea
  • Chest pain or discomfort; possible AMI
  • Hypotension (SBP <90 mmHg)
  • CHF / Pulmonary edema
  • Heart rate >160 bpm with wide QRS >0.12 sec and “twisting of points” appearance

EMR

  1. Administer 100% oxygen
  2. Maintain airway as appropriate
  3. Pulse oximetry
  4. Supportive care

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry
  2. Glucose check

AEMT

  1. IV or INT with Normal Saline TKO
  2. Titrate Dextrose 50% PRN slowly to normalize blood glucose

Paramedic

  1. Obtain and transmit 12-lead EKG
  2. If systolic BP <90 mmHg:
    1. Prepare for synchronized cardioversion at 100 joules; escalate as needed
    2. Consider sedation:
      • Valium 2–5 mg IV (Peds: 0.2 mg/kg)
      • OR Versed 2–5 mg IV (Peds: 0.1 mg/kg)
    3. Consider pain control per adult pain management protocol
    4. Administer Magnesium Sulfate 1–2 grams IV push over 2 minutes
    5. If rate >160 bpm – contact Medical Control
    6. Consider Amiodarone 150–300 mg IV/IO over 10 minutes (Peds: 5 mg/kg)
  3. If systolic BP >90 mmHg:
    1. Administer Magnesium Sulfate 1–2 grams IV push over 2 minutes
    2. Repeat Magnesium if rate remains elevated (<160 bpm)
    3. If rate >160 bpm – contact Medical Control
    4. Consider Amiodarone 150–300 mg IV/IO over 10 minutes (Peds: 5 mg/kg)
