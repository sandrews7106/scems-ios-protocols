SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

113 – POST RESUSCITATION

Assessment

  • Completion of arrhythmia treatment

EMT

  1. Oxygen 100% and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition
  2. Glucose check (EMT Skill)
  3. Supportive care

AEMT

  1. IV NS TKO
  2. If systolic < 90 mmHg, administer 250 ml NS bolus
    (Pediatrics: 70 + 2×age; 20 cc/kg bolus); repeat until BP is > 90 mmHg or appropriate for age
  3. If blood sugar is low, titrate Dextrose 50% PRN slowly to avoid large glucose swings

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit
  2. If anti-arrhythmic was administered:
    1. Amiodarone: 300 mg IV (peds 5 mg/kg, may repeat ×3); second dose 150 mg if arrhythmia persists
    2. Lidocaine: If used, start infusion at 2–4 mg/min
  3. Continue ventilatory support to maintain ETCO₂ > 20; keep respirations < 12/min ideally
  4. If ET tube not tolerated, refer to sedation protocol
  5. Consider RSI
  6. Use soft restraints if needed for safety (to prevent extubation)
