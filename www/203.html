SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

203 – ELECTROCUTION / LIGHTNING INJURIES

Assessment

  • Presence of signs and symptoms of electrical injury
  • Entry / exit wounds

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition
  2. Spinal protection if electrocution/lightning over 1000 volts or suspicion of spinal injury
  3. Supportive care
  4. Treat burn per burn protocol

EMT

  1. Control any gross hemorrhage and dress wounds
  2. Pulse oximetry

AEMT

  1. IV Crystalloid Solution (LR/NS) if signs of shock 20 cc/kg bolus of fluid
    (peds: 20 cc/kg bolus)

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit
  2. Consider 2nd IV en route to hospital
  3. Consider pain management protocol
