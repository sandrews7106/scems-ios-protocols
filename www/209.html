SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

209 – RADIATION/HAZMAT

Assessment

  • Extent of radiation/chemical exposure (number of victims, skin vs. inhalation exposure)
  • Nature of exposure
  • Symptoms exhibited by patient
  • Neurologic status (LOC, pupil size)
  • General appearance (dry or sweaty skin, flushed, cyanotic, singed hair)
  • Associated injuries
  • Decontamination prior to treatment

EMR

  1. If eye exposure, irrigate for a minimum of 20 minutes
  2. Treat associated injuries (LSB, limb immobilization, wound treatment)
  3. Supportive care
  4. Treat per burn protocol
  5. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry (keep sats >94%)

AEMT

  1. INT or IV NS/LR, if hypotensive 20 cc/kg (peds 20 cc/kg)

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit
