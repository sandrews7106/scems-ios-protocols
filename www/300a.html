SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

300A – MEDICAL COMPLAINT NOT SPECIFIED UNDER OTHER PROTOCOLS

Assessment

  • Pertinent history related to complaint
  • Allergies and medications taken or prescribed
  • Provocation
  • Quality of pain or discomfort
  • Relieved by
  • Signs and symptoms
  • Onset, type, and duration of pain

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition
  2. Position patient appropriately for condition
  3. Supportive care

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry
  2. Glucose check

AEMT

  1. INT or IV NS TKO, unless signs of shock; then give 20 cc/kg fluid bolus

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit if indicated
