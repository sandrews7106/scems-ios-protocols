SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

304B – PREHOSPITAL SCREEN FOR THROMBOLYTIC THERAPY

Instructions

  • Complete this report for all patients symptomatic for Acute Coronary Syndrome or CVA.
  • Report to the Emergency Department Physician/Nurse any positive findings.
  • Document all findings in the PCR.

Checklist

  • Witness/next of kin contact info: ____________________________
  • Time of onset of the symptoms: ____________________________
  • □ Systolic BP >240 mmHg
  • □ Diastolic BP >110 mmHg
  • □ Right arm vs. Left arm Systolic BP difference >15 mmHg
  • □ History of recent brain/spinal cord surgery, CVA, or injury
  • □ Recent trauma or surgery
  • □ Bleeding disorder that causes the patient to bleed excessively
  • □ Prolonged CPR (>10 minutes)
  • □ Pregnancy
  • □ Taking Coumadin, Aspirin, or other blood thinners
