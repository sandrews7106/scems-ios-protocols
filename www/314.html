SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

314 – SEXUAL ASSAULT

Assessment

  • Traumatic Injuries

All Levels

  • Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to patient’s condition
  • Be calm and assuring with sensitivity toward the patient
  • DO NOT make unnecessary physical contact with the patient
  • If possible, have a witness the same gender as the victim present at all times
  • Wrap a plastic sheet around the victim if possible
  • DO NOT inspect genitals unless evidence of uncontrolled hemorrhage, trauma, or severe pain is present
  • DO NOT allow patient to shower or douche
  • Collect patient’s clothing when possible
    • Place clothing in plastic sheet or separate plastic/paper bags with ID labels and found location
    • Leave all sheets placed in plastic/paper bag with patient at facility
    • Notify all staff of clothing samples
  • Transport patient to appropriate facility for treatment and examination
  • Contact dispatch to notify Police of possible Sexual Assault
