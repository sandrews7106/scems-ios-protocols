SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

315 – SICKLE CELL ANEMIA

Assessment

  • History of Sickle Cell Anemia
  • Signs of infection
  • Hypoxia
  • Dehydration
  • Painful joint(s)
  • Limited movement of joint

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to patient’s condition
  2. Supportive care

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry (keep oxygen sats >95%)

AEMT

  1. IV NS bolus 20 cc/kg (peds: 10–20 cc/kg bolus)

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit if appropriate
  2. If pain persists – consider pain management protocol
