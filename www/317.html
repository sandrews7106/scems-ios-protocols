SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

317 – SYNCOPE

Assessment

  • Loss of consciousness with recovery
  • Lightheadedness, dizziness
  • Palpitations, slow or rapid pulse, irregular pulse
  • Decreased blood pressure

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition
  2. Supportive care

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry
  2. Glucose check

AEMT

  1. INT or IV NS TKO – if hypotensive, 20 cc/kg bolus (peds: 20 cc/kg bolus)
  2. Titrate Dextrose 50% PRN slowly until normal glucose levels achieved. Avoid large serum glucose swings.

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG – treat any cardiac dysrhythmia per appropriate protocol
  2. Assess neurological status – if abnormal, refer to appropriate protocol
