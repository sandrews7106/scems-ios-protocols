SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

405 – EYE TRAUMA

Assessment

  • Impaled object
  • Inability to open eye(s)
  • Swollen, edematous eye(s)
  • Photophobia
  • Visual defects, loss of vision
  • Redness

EMR / EMT / AEMT / Paramedic

  1. Treatment – Standing Order
  2. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate for patient’s condition
  3. C-Spine protection if needed
  4. If thermal or chemical:
    1. Flush eye(s) with NS or water for 15 minutes
    2. Cover both eyes
    3. Transport
  5. Penetration:
    1. Stabilize
    2. Cover unaffected eye
    3. Transport
  6. Blunt trauma:
    1. Cover both eyes
    2. Transport
  7. Is loss of vision present:
    1. No – Contact Medical Control
    2. Yes – If loss of vision was sudden, painless, and non-traumatic, consider Retinal Artery Occlusion. Contact Medical Control and:
      1. Apply cardiac monitor and assess for changes (EMT and above only)
