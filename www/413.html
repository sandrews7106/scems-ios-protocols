SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

413 – SPINAL CORD INJURIES

Assessment

  • Hypotension without acute volume loss
  • Warm/flushed skin despite hypotension
  • Paralysis
  • Loss of reflexes
  • Posturing
  • Priapism
  • Diaphragmatic breathing

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate for the patient’s condition
  2. C-Spine protection
  3. Control hemorrhaging

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry

AEMT

  1. IV fluid NS or LR. If hypotensive, give bolus 20 cc/kg (repeat once if needed)

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit if appropriate
  2. Treatment – Protocol:
    1. Consider Dopamine 2–20 µg/kg/min OR
    2. Epinephrine 2–10 mcg over 1 min, may be repeated in 5 min
