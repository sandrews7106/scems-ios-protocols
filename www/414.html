SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

414 – TRAUMATIC CARDIAC ARREST

Assessment

  • Cardiac arrest secondary to trauma

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate for the patient’s condition
  2. CPR

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry

AEMT

  1. IV NS/LR give 20 cc/kg bolus
  2. Consider second IV access

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit if appropriate
  2. Treat cardiac rhythms per specific protocols
  3. If suspected pneumothorax perform needle chest decompression
  4. Consider viability of patient prior to transport
