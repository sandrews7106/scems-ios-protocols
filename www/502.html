SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

502 – ABRUPTIO PLACENTA

Assessment

  • Multiparity
  • Maternal hypertension
  • Trauma
  • Drug use
  • Increased maternal age
  • History
  • Vaginal bleeding with no increase in pain
  • No bleeding with low abdominal pain

EMR

  1. Oxygen and airway maintenance appropriate to the patient’s condition
  2. Position patient in the left lateral recumbent position

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry

AEMT

  1. IV NS TKO, if hypotensive 20 cc/kg (peds 20 cc/kg)

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG (If appropriate)
