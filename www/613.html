SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

613 - NEONATAL RESUSCITATION

Assessment

  • Newborn with respiratory or circulatory distress

EMR

  1. Dry and place in face up head down position
  2. Keep infant level with mother until cord is clamped
  3. Suction airway, if obvious obstruction to spontaneous breathing or requiring positive pressure ventilation
  4. Respirations
    1. If spontaneous
      1. Wait 1-2 minutes then complete clamping cord and cut between clamps
      2. Cover infant head
      3. Wrap and keep warm
      4. Provide Oxygen
      5. Transport without delay
    2. If no respirations
      1. Stimulate respirations: rub back, slap bottom of feet gently, if no change or respirations become depressed (<20 bpm)
      2. Re-suction airway
      3. High flow oxygen if no change ventilate with BVM at 30/min
      4. Wait 1-2 minutes then clamp cord and cut between clamps
      5. Transport Immediately
  5. Pulse
    1. If pulse rate is less than 60 perform CPR at rate of 120 compressions/min, transport
    2. Continue chest compressions

EMT

  1. Pulse oximetry

AEMT

  1. INT or IV NS, if hypotensive bolus 20 cc/kg
  2. If pulse rate is >60 keep warm, ventilate with BVM if necessary, transport

Paramedic

  1. The dose of epinephrine is 0.01 mg/kg IV/IO (0.1 cc/kg of 1:10,000) given q 3-5 minutes, and repeat until heart rate is above 60/minute. Refer to the length based tape to confirm dosage.
  2. 12 Lead EKG, if appropriate
