SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

703 – CHEST DECOMPRESSION

Paramedic

Procedure

  1. Cleanse skin on affected side using aseptic technique
  2. Using a 14 or 16 gauge 3½” angiocath, insert between the 2nd/3rd rib mid-clavicular or 4th/5th rib mid-axillary spaces
  3. Advance needle until “pop” is felt while entering the pleural space
  4. Advance catheter until hub contacts skin
  5. Cover catheter hub with Asherman Chest Seal or similar device (ensure one-way valve effect)
  6. Reassess patient for breath sound changes
  7. If signs of tension reoccur, check chest seal and consider repeating chest decompression using above steps
  8. Contact Medical Control
  9. Transport

Notes

  • Use the same procedure for pediatric patients: use 18 or 20 gauge angiocath
