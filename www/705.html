SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

705 – CANCELLATION (SIGNAL 9) POLICY

  1. Sullivan County EMS and Sullivan County First Responder units dispatched by 911 centers are to be cancelled only by the following:
    1. 911 Dispatch Center or
    2. Responding Sullivan County EMS unit
  2. Appropriately trained personnel on scene:
    1. Patient refuses care and appropriate release obtained (unless altered mental status or signs of serious illness/injury)*
    2. Patient does not refuse care:
      1. Emergency Medical Responder level or above.
      2. Must have necessary equipment on scene to assess and provide care for patient.
      3. Patient assessment complete.
      4. Patient must be stable and show no signs of life threatening illness or injury.*
      5. Extrication not required
      6. Person cancelling responding units must identify self by personal call sign or name. Individual cancelling response accepts all responsibility for their actions.
    3. No patient/accident found after arriving on scene/in area.
  3. Paramedic ON SCENE may cancel additional response at their discretion

Notes

  • This policy does not apply to the occasional need to divert a unit to other emergency traffic.
  • Nor does it apply to a responder’s ability to “back down” other responding units to “routine” response when indicated.
