SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

717 – PPE DOFFING PROCEDURE

Sullivan County EMS PPE Doffing Procedure

  1. Trained Observer will have doffing pad in place.
  2. Engage TO. If the TO touches the HCW PPE, they must immediately perform hand hygiene and allow to dry.
  3. Inspect outer garment for visible contamination, cuts, or tears. Disinfect if needed and document issues.
  4. Disinfect outer gloves with bleach wipe or hand sanitizer and allow to dry.
  5. Before exiting room, remove apron and discard by rolling from inside to outside to avoid contamination.
  6. Disinfect outer gloves again and allow to dry.
  7. Inspect PPE for contamination or damage. Disinfect if needed. Document any issues.
  8. Disinfect gloved hands again with bleach wipe or sanitizer.
  9. Remove boot covers. If cut by TO, HCW steps out carefully. TO disinfects hands afterward.
  10. Disinfect inner gloves and allow to dry.
  11. Step out of the room onto the doffing pad with red bag receptacle nearby.
  12. Inspect PPE again. Disinfect and document issues.
  13. Disinfect hands again and allow to dry.
  14. Remove outer gloves using glove-in-glove technique. Discard in trash.
  15. Inspect inner gloves. Perform hand hygiene. Replace if needed. Document issues.
  16. TO assists with gown removal. Roll gown inside-out, touching only inner surface. Discard and document.
  17. Disinfect inner gloves and allow to dry.
  18. Inspect Tyvek suit. Disinfect if needed. Document issues.
  19. Disinfect inner gloves again and allow to dry.
  20. Remove Tyvek suit carefully. TO may assist.
  21. Perform hand hygiene. TO disinfects if assisting.
  22. Remove face shield by leaning forward and pulling away from face.
  23. Disinfect inner gloves and allow to dry.
  24. Remove hood by pulling from the top forward while leaning forward.
  25. Disinfect inner gloves and allow to dry.
  26. Remove N95 by pinching and pulling away from face while leaning forward.
  27. Disinfect inner gloves and allow to dry.
  28. Remove blue bonnet by pulling away while leaning forward.
  29. TO disinfects one shoe, HCW steps off pad, repeat for second shoe. HCW performs hand hygiene.
  30. Disinfect and remove final gloves with glove-in-glove technique. Discard in trash.
  31. HCW and TO perform final hand hygiene.
  32. Inspect HCW for contamination. If found, disinfect, notify Infection Control, and document.
  33. Move to designated room, remove clothing without passing over face. Cut if needed.
  34. Shower for at least 5 minutes using soap and shampoo.

Notes

  • TO should remove doffing pads used inside the room, roll inward, and dispose. Follow doffing procedure afterward.
  • Personal glasses should be disinfected with bleach wipes, allowed to dry, then rinsed.
  • Any breach of PPE should prompt immediate disinfection and supervisor notification.
