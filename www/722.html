SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

722 – SEDATION

Assessment

  • Transcutaneous Pacing
  • Cardioversion
  • CPAP
  • Traumatic injury patient where extrication or movement will cause severe pain
  • Mechanical Ventilation

EMR / EMT

  1. Oxygen to maintain a SPO2 of 94%
  2. Supportive care

AEMT

  1. INT or IV NS, if hypotensive 20 cc/kg

Paramedic

  1. 12 Lead EKG, transmit if appropriate
  2. Place patient on capnography prior to sedation if time allows, but MUST be on after patient has been sedated
  3. Procedural sedation:
    • For short duration sedation, Etomidate 10mg (preferred for cardioversion) – Adult
    • 2mg/kg Ketamine IV – if no effect, may repeat dose or continue to the following
    • 2.5–5mg Versed IV/IN
    • 1–2mg Ativan IV
  4. Perform procedure while monitoring patient respiratory status
