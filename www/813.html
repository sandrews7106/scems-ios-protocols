SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

813 – DRUG INFUSION ADMIX DOSAGE GUIDELINES

PARAMEDIC

Lidocaine:

2 gram medication/500 mL D5W = 4 mg/mL (always use 60 gtt. set)

  • 1 mg/min = 15 gtt/min
  • 2 mg/min = 30 gtt/min
  • 3 mg/min = 45 gtt/min
  • 4 mg/min = 60 gtt/min

Procainamide:

For maintenance infusion only. Refer to Specific Standing Order for Initial Dose

2 gram medication/500 mL D5W = 4 mg/mL or 1 gm/250 cc D5W (always use 60 gtt. set)

  • 1 mg/min = 15 gtt/min
  • 2 mg/min = 30 gtt/min
  • 3 mg/min = 45 gtt/min
  • 4 mg/min = 60 gtt/min

Magnesium Sulfate:

4 gram in 250 cc D5W (16 mg/mL) run at 30–60 gtt/min

Dopamine:

400 mg / 250 cc D5W or 800 mg / 500 cc D5W = 1600 mcg/mL (always use 60 gtt. set)
