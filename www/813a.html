SULLIVAN COUNTY EMS PROTOCOLS

813A – PUSH DOSE EPI

How to make it

  1. Discard 1ml out of a pre-filled saline flush.
  2. Replace the 1ml with 1ml of EPI 1:10.
  3. You now have a 10ml syringe with 100mcg of EPI.

How to give it and how much

  1. It should only be given IV/IO
  2. The dosing is 2–10mcg over 1–2 minutes.
  3. **The MOST you should ever give is 1ml.**
